The Mighty Chole Bature

Doesn't matter where are you travelling in India. You will always have this as breakfast.

Chole Bhature is a combination of chana masala and fried bread called bhatura made of maida from India. Chole Bhatura is a world famous recipe from the Punjabi Cuisine. Chole is a spicy curry made from white chickpeas where as Bhatura is a fried leavened bread.

Both chole and bhatura are two separate dishes eaten together to give a delicious tangy taste. This recipe cannot be categorized under one such category. Chole Bhature can fall under fast food, street food, complete and as a breakfast item.

Be it street or a posh restaurant, they always serve this dish accompanied by sweetend curd drink called Lassi. A hot plate of Chole Bhature is a sheer treat.


The Legendary Kothu Parota

Kothu Parota is the quintessential breakfast for any South Indian.

Kothu parotta which is literally minced parotta, is a delicacy popular in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is made using parotta, egg/ meat/veggies, and salna, a spicy sauce. The varieties include Vegetarian, Chilli, Egg and Non Vegetarian.

The ingredients are thrown on a hot cast-iron griddle lubricated with oil. These are minced and mixed by repeated pounding using a heavy iron spatula. It is usually served with some curry and onion raita.

This is famous for it being a street food. They may be available in restaurants but are usually considered better in street food shops.
